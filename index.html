The CloCoffee Difference: More Than Just a Coffee Shop

by Meghna Bathi

‘"‘It’s so watery.’ ‘Why is there a glob of gunk?’ Frustrated with subpar coffee, our reporter ventured into Edison and found CloCoffee. Unlike mainstream shops, CloCoffee prioritizes quality, using reverse osmosis water and offering diverse milk alternatives. ‘People are smiling in there!’ And for good reason. With quiet, rentable rooms, a welcoming atmosphere where ‘the staff doesn’t yell the order numbers, but brings the drink to each person individually,’ and a commitment to kindness, CloCoffee, owned by Ralph Angeles, redefines the coffee shop experience. Daily D&G explores this local gem."
